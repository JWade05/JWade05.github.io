About Me

My name is Jason Wade. For as long as I can remember, I've always had an avid interest in technology; specifically its ability to communicate with people, and especially in a visual medium. My main interests have always lied in animation and the arts, and as I began to work more in those mediums, I started interacting with more technology and all that it has to offer. Below are examples of various art that I have created both traditionally and otherwise.
